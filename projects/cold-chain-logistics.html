Cold-Chain Logistics

Thermal Management Platform

Management software architecture for cold-chain logistics, deployed on harizeon.com.

Overview

A comprehensive platform for managing temperature-sensitive logistics operations with real-time monitoring and alerts.

Deployed commercially on harizeon.com with production-grade infrastructure and monitoring capabilities.

Key Features

Temperature Monitoring

Real-time tracking of thermal conditions across shipments.

Alert System

Instant notifications for temperature deviations and anomalies.

GPS Tracking

Full logistics route tracking with real-time updates.

Analytics

Detailed reports and performance metrics for logistics optimization.

Team Management

Multi-user support with role-based access control.

Cloud Integration

AWS-powered infrastructure for scalability and reliability.

Demo

Simulate shipment temperature to test alert thresholds.
